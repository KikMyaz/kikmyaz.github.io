Pass Plus
Night Driving
General


-When driving in and out of built up areas you may pass through lit and unlit roads. Be careful as your eyes adjust to each phase.

-When exiting brightly lit service areas, be careful as your eyes readjust to the dark.

-Be extra careful overtaking at night. It can be very difficult to assess the road ahead.

-Avoid making noise at night. Don’t rev the engine unnecessarily or slam car doors. The horn shouldn’t be used after 11.30 or before 7am except to avoid danger from an oncoming vehicle. Flash your headlights instead, to indicate your presence.

- Night time driving in the rain can reduce visibility significantly, especially out of side windows where raindrops on the glass will mottle the light confusingly.
