Driving Test Nerves

Most people will suffer from nerves leading up to their driving test. It is not a pleasant feeling but a little anxiety can be helpful in keeping you alert and improving your performance.

A good nights sleep and a healthy breakfast should set you in good stead for your test but if you find your nerves are particularly difficult to manage, consider:

Medicine

Bach Rescue Remedy can be taken as a spray, capsule, drops or gum. Kalms, which is a herbal remedy, might need starting a few weeks before your test.

Breathing Excercises

Long, peaceful breaths cue your mind and body that everything is ok. Short, sharp breaths do the opposite. By breathing correctly, you can tell your body how to feel.

Yoga or Tai Chi

These eastern techniques utilise physical and mental excercises which can relax you and channel your energy to positive outcomes.
