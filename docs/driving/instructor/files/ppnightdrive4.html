Night Driving
Pedestrians and Cyclists


-Pedestrians and cyclists are not compelled to wear reflective clothing at night. Be extra vigilant especially in areas where street lighting and shadows merge.

-Pedestrians might be more likely to run into roads unexpectedly when it is raining. They may be in a rush to reach the dry or not see properly due to hoods and umbrellas. Your visibility will be reduced due to mottled light through the water on the windows. Adjust your speed and be extra vigilant in these conditions.

-Slow down and be extra cautious around pubs and club at night where pedestrians can be unpredictable.
