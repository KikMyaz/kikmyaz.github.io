Sunshine and Hot Weather


-Make sure there is enough coolant in the system before a long journey. Overheated engines in hot conditions are the most frequent cause of breakdowns.

-Also make sure there is water in the screenwash reservoir. Use an additive to help clear the windscreen of flies and insects as there are generally more about in hot weather.

-Check tyre pressures before your journey, whilst they are still cold.

-Glare can seriously affect your visibility so use your sun visor where appropriate and consider using sun glasses. This can also be a problem in the winter when the sun is low in the sky.

-Tarmac roads can become soft in extreme heat so take care when braking and cornering.

-Use adequate ventilation when driving in hot weather. A hot, stuffy environment can increase tiredness.
