Breakdown on the Carriageway

Most dual carriageways don't have a wide hard shoulder or regular emergency telephones as on a motorway.

If you break down on a dual carriageway you should:

  • Try to get safely into a layby or pull as far onto the grass verge as you can.
  • Turn on your hazard warning lights
  • Place a warning triangle about 45metres behind the vehicle and well off of the carriageway.
  • Keep animals inside the car
  • If the vehicle is in any way exposed to the traffic flow, direct yourself and all passengers to exit by the nearside doors and wait on the bank.
  • Call for assistance
