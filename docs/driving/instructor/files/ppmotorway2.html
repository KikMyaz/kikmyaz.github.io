Motorway Driving
Motorway Regulations


-Parking is forbidden except at service areas.


Motorways must not be used by:

-Pedestrians

-Cyclists

-Horse riders

-Provisional licence holders. (You need a full licence)

-Motorcycles under 50cc

-Slow moving vehicles with oversized loads (Except with special permission).

-Invalid carriages less than 254kg unladen weight

-Agricultural vehicles
