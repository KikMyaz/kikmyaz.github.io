Night Driving
Parking at Night


-Normal, safe, legal and convenient considerations apply.

-On roads with speed limits of 30mph or less you can park without lights, facing the direction of traffic flow, not within 10 metres (32 feet) of a junction. You must also comply with parking restrictions.

-On roads with a speed limit of 40mph or more, parking lights must be used and the same rules as above apply.

-Turn headlights off when parked even for a short while. Fixed headlights can be dazzling especially, if for any reason, the car is parked on the offside of the road.
