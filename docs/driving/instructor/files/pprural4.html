Rural Roads
Reading Road Surfaces


Country roads can be particularly liable to poor surface conditions. Look out for:

-Mud washed on from the verge or dropped from agricultural vehicles.

-Rainfall after a long dry spell makes a layer of leaves or plant material extra slippy.

-Loose road surface on a gravel or dirt road.

-Potholes.

-Uneven roads or ones with steep cambers.

-Soft tarmac on hot days.

Use appropriate caution in these conditions.
