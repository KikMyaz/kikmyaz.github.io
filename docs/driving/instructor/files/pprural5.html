Rural Roads
Soft Verges


-Where in the city, most roads are bordered with kerbstone, in rural areas, the camber of the road most often falls away into a soft (grassy or earth) verge. Where roads are narrow and potential speeds are high there is a danger of straying over the edges of the road and a real risk of losing control. Extra care must be made to keep to the road especially when it becomes narrow through meeting traffic. If you do need to venture a little onto the soft verge do so at a very low speed.
