Night Driving
Dazzling


-The main beam is very useful to see when there is no street lighting. However it can seriously dazzle other road users.

-Make sure you dip your headlights when encountering any oncoming traffic.

-Turn off your main beam when following traffic.

-Remember to turn off your main beam after being overtaken.

-Make sure that even dipped headlights fall short of the rear of the vehicle you’re following. Don’t travel too close.

-Fog lights dazzle. Use appropriately.

-Use handbrake at night when stopped, as opposed to footbrake. Brakelights can dazzle in standing traffic.

-If dazzled by oncoming traffic, slow down and stop if necessary. Don’t retaliate with full beam.

-Weight in the back of the car can cause dazzle for other road users. Use headlight adjusters if they’re available.
