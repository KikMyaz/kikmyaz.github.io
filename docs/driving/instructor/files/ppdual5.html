Dual Carriageway
Overtaking.


-Unlike on motorways, slow moving vehicles like tractors and pushbikes can use the dual carriageway. Make sure you pass these with plenty of space and slow down a little if necessary.

It can be unsafe to overtake when:

  • Approaching roundabouts and junctions
  • Fast moving traffic is approaching from behind
  • Approaching a slip road where traffic is merging
  • Where the speed limit decreases
