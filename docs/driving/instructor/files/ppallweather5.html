All Weather Driving
Wind


-Your vehicle can become unstable in strong crosswinds, especially on open stretches of road. You may feel a pull on the steering wheel or the vehicle may wander. Be prepared to slow down or avoid these kinds of roads in such conditions.

-High sided vehicles are particularly vulnerable to windy conditions. Give them extra space when overtaking.

-Cyclists and motorcyclists, too, are particularly vulnerable. They may suddenly veer into your path so allow them extra room.
