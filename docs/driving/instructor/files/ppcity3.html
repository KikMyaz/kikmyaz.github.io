Spacial Awareness and Clearance


Always maintain safe clearances around your vehicle on all sides:

-Ahead-Maintain safe stopping distance from the vehicle in front as your speed increases and decreases. When stopped in traffic remember to leave enough of a gap that you could get round if the car in front broke down.

-Behind-Regularly check your mirrors for following vehicles. Use extra caution ahead if a vehicle is tailgating you.

-Left-Keep to the left of the road but maintain a distance from the kerb or parked cars. If you have to pass close then slow down.

-Right-Stay to your side of the centre of the road. If you have to cross the line, use caution.
