Eco Safe Driving In Towns

-Always keep within the speed limit. This is important for safety, but it also means your vehicle burns less fuel than even a few MPH faster.

-Use the accelerator smoothly. Over revving and pulling away fast usually achieves nothing but using more fuel.

-Use the highest practical gear. Modern cars have high power ratios so can travel quite slowly in a higher gear. However busy roads where there is a requirement to change speeds regularly, a lower gear is more suitable.

-To make efficient progress, use early observation and good anticipation. Approaching a red light slowly could mean that you don’t need to stop and start. Approaching a green light cautiously could mean that you don’t need to brake too hard if it turns to red.

-Skipping gears can reduce the time you need spend accelerating. Practice this where possible.
