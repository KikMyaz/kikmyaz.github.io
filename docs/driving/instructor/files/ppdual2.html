Roundabouts

-Observe any advance warning signs of roundabouts and slow down in plenty of time.

-If you'd been travelling fast and in fifth gear, you may need to change down to fourth or third as you reduce speed.

-Read the sign to get a clear idea of your route direction

-Read road markings and arrows and get into the appropriate lane as early as possible. Don't straddle lanes or change at the last minute.

-Deal with the roundabout as described.
