Filter Lights 
1.Without the filter light illuminated, the learner car turning right is giving way to oncoming traffic as usual


2.An illuminated filter arrow gives you priority in that direction. The oncoming traffic will face a red light


3.In this example the right turners are responding to the filter arrow and proceeding. The orange car is responding to the red light and stopping
