Pocket Instructor offers three useful sections:

1.Driving Lessons
Starting with the initial cockpit drill and controls lesson, the Driving Lesson section proceeds through sixteen broad subject areas all the way through to the day of your driving test.

The information is delivered in a similar format as you might expect on your driving lessons and is designed to be a convenient reference point to prepare for or reflect upon them.

Though most information should be consistent with your lesson content, emphasis and technique may vary, so listen carefully to your instructor and engage them in discussion where you are not clear.

It is recommended to take this course and practice important elements of it with an approved driving instructor. These notes should assist you in doing so.

3.Road Templates
This library of junctions and lane layouts is designed to illustrate some of the subtle differences between them. Perhaps they will help you re-play and clarify lesson and test incidents.

The feature can be particularly useful if your phone has a screen capture and drawing function.
